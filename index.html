About Us

GeoTerrasia (Terrestrial Surveying for Indonesia) was founded in 2025 by Group 1 from the Survey and Mapping Management course. GeoTerrasia focuses on topography, utilizing modern technologies such as GNSS, drone mapping, photogrammetry, and GIS software. We support various sectors including construction, mining, energy, and spatial planning. With a competent and experienced team, GeoTerrasia is committed to being a strategic partner in delivering innovative and high-quality topographic survey and mapping solutions..
